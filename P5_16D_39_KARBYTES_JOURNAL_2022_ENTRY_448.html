KARBYTES_JOURNAL_2022_ENTRY_448

I am pleased with my my most recent drum recording because my timing and “energy” has improved (which consequently makes for a more enjoyable listening experience which flows better and which feels less anxiety inducing like my older recordings sound to me). I had a surprising amount of fun using my homemade MP3 player to play multiple sound tracks simultaneously to create a quasi song (which entertained me and helped me stay focused while I worked on the SOUND_TRACK_LOOP_COUNTER page of Karlina Object dot WordPress dot Com).
